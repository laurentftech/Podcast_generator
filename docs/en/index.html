🎙Podcast Generator 🎙

A lightweight desktop app with a modern UI that lets you generate multi-speaker podcasts from any script using high-quality voices from **ElevenLabs** and **Google Gemini**.

How It Works

1. Get API Key

Grab a free key from ElevenLabs or Google Gemini and add it securely to the app.

2. Write Script

Format your text with speaker names (e.g., John: Hello world!).

3. Generate

Click a button to create your high-quality podcast in MP3 or WAV format.

Main Features

  • ✨ **Modern UI**: A clean and responsive interface that adapts to your system's light or dark mode.
  • 🎤 **Dual TTS Provider**: Choose between the high-quality voices of **Google Gemini** or **ElevenLabs**.
  • 📄 **Synchronized HTML Demo**: Automatically generate a shareable HTML page with your podcast audio and a synchronized, highlighted transcript.
  • 🔐 **Secure & Simple**: Your API keys are stored securely in your system's keychain, and the app guides you through the setup.
  • 🎨 **Voice Customization**: Easily configure and save voices for each speaker in your scripts, with built-in voice guides and sample playback.
